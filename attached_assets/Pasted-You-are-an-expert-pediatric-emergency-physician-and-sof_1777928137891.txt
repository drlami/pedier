🧠 STEP 2: LOGIC ENGINE

The calculator must follow this EXACT DECISION TREE:

🔴 RULE 1: RED FLAGS OVERRIDE EVERYTHING

IF any red flag OR child is NOT well-appearing:
→ CLASSIFY as HIGH RISK
→ OUTPUT:

“Suspected serious bacterial infection”
Immediate FULL SEPSIS WORKUP
Admit to hospital

LOW RISK CRITERIA:
Temperature < 39°C
Fever < 48 hours
Fully immunized
No SBI risk factors

→ MANAGEMENT:

No labs required
No antibiotics
Discharge with safety net
Follow-up in 24–48 hours
INTERMEDIATE / HIGHER RISK:

IF ANY of the following:

Temp ≥ 39°C
Fever > 48h
Age < 12 months
Uncircumcised male
Female < 24 months
No clear source

→ DO:

URINE TEST (MANDATORY)
Consider blood tests
