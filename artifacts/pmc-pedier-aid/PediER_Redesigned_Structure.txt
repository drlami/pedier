PediER Aid — Redesigned ER Structure
Based on: Fleisher & Ludwig (8th Ed.), APLS (7th Ed.), Nelson Symptom-Based Diagnosis (2nd Ed.)
Last updated: 2026-06-30

PRINCIPLE
---------
All categories are organized by what the doctor SEES at the door (chief complaint),
not by the diagnosis. A doctor in a busy ER should open one category and find the protocol
in under 10 seconds.

Legend
  [EXISTS]   = already built or registered in the app
  [NEW]      = not yet in the app, needs to be added
  [MOVE]     = exists but is in the wrong category currently

    Acute Renal Failure

    All 11 topics merged into one category

After these moves, "Focal Infections" and "Electrolyte Disorders"
as standalone categories can be retired entirely.
